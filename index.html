RPM Governor

A printer circuit board, which heart is a microcontroller ATmega328, the circuit board was disgned in Eagle and the Software written in VisualStudio with the plugin VisualMicro. The factory JLCPCB manofacture all the gerber files. its principal aim is read the RPM of the engine also the temperature, the status of the lights, the voltage of the battery and by consequence the health of the Alternator. Whether the RPM surpass 3000RPM the system produce an alarm alerting to the driver, if the driver carry on and the engine transgress 3250 RPM the system open a relay and turn off the engine.
